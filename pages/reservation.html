Reservation Information

We look forward to welcoming you to Bella Italia. To ensure the best possible dining experience, please review our reservation policies below.

Reservation Policies

  • Reservations are recommended, especially for dinner and weekends.
  • For parties of 8 or more, please call us directly at 964-622-3903 to make a reservation.
  • We hold reservations for 15 minutes past the scheduled time. After that, tables may be released to accommodate waiting guests.
  • If you need to cancel or modify your reservation, please do so at least 24 hours in advance.

Seating Options

We offer several seating options to enhance your dining experience:

  • Main Dining Room: Elegant and comfortable atmosphere
  • Garden Terrace: Al fresco dining surrounded by greenery (weather permitting)
  • Private Dining Room: Available for special occasions and larger parties
  • Bar Area: Casual seating with full menu service

Please indicate your seating preference in the Special Requests field, and we will do our best to accommodate you.
