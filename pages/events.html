Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book my event?

We recommend booking at least 4-6 weeks in advance for private events, especially for weekend dates. For larger events or during peak seasons (holidays, summer), 2-3 months advance booking is advisable.

Is there a minimum guest count or spending requirement?

Minimum requirements vary depending on the event space, day of the week, and time of year. The Tuscany Room typically requires a minimum of 10 guests, while the Venetian Hall requires a minimum of 25 guests. Please contact our events team for specific details.

Can you accommodate dietary restrictions and allergies?

Yes, we can accommodate most dietary restrictions and allergies with advance notice. Please inform our events team of any special dietary needs when planning your event, and we will work with you to create suitable menu options.

Can I bring my own cake for a special occasion?

Yes, you may bring your own cake for special occasions. There is a cake cutting fee of $2 per person. Alternatively, our pastry chef can create a custom cake for your event with advance notice.

Do you provide decorations for private events?

We provide standard table settings, linens, and candles. For additional decorations, we can work with you or your event planner. Any decorations must be approved in advance and should not damage our facilities.
