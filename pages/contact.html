Frequently Asked Questions

Do you take reservations?

Yes, we highly recommend making a reservation, especially for dinner and weekends. You can book a table online through our website or by calling us directly.

Do you offer vegetarian and gluten-free options?

Yes, we have a variety of vegetarian and gluten-free options on our menu. Please inform your server about any dietary restrictions or allergies.

Is there parking available?

We have a dedicated parking lot for our guests. Valet parking is also available during dinner hours on Friday and Saturday.

Do you host private events?

Yes, we have a private dining room that can accommodate up to 30 guests. Please contact us for more information about hosting your special event at Bella Italia.
